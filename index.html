About Me


I'm a fourth-year PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Alyosha Efros. Previously, I did my undergrad at MIT where I worked with Antonio Torralba. My research focuses on deep learning and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on deep generative models. I've interned at FAIR, Adobe Research and NVIDIA. I'm supported by the National Science Foundation's Graduate Research Fellowship Program.
